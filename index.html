Monstera albo facts you may not know

The Mostera albo was discover by Botanist Charles Plumier in 1693. Another ineteresting fact is that the variegation on the Monstera albo can be quite unstable due to its natural mutation. As many plant the varigation on this plant can reverse. However, there is a way to to bring the variation back. The trick to keep the variegation going is with medium to high light, high humidity, water the plant every time the top 4 inches of soil gets dry.

Monsters albo Care

The monstera albo will need bright light. Place this plant in an area that receives indirect or filtered sunlight all day long. Avoid placing the albo in direct sunlight as this will burn its leaves. The variegated pieces of the leaves are extra susceptible to sunburn.

Propagating Monstera albo

There are a few different ways to propagate an albo monstera plant. The safest way to propagete will be by stem cutting. Since they are easy to root and prom to get root rode is better after you cut the stem to place it in purify water or in LECA.
